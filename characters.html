Marvel Characters

Iron Man

Genius billionaire Tony Stark builds the Iron Man suit and becomes one of the founding members of the Avengers. His sacrifice in Endgame marks one of the most heroic moments in the MCU.

Captain America

Steve Rogers, the super soldier and symbol of hope, led the Avengers through thick and thin. His journey ends with a peaceful closure after returning the Infinity Stones.

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff, the master spy and acrobat, sacrificed herself to save the universe. Her legacy lives on through the upcoming generation of heroes.

Doctor Strange

The Sorcerer Supreme manipulates time and reality, playing a key role in the Multiverse saga. His decisions affect more than one universe.

Wanda Maximoff

The Scarlet Witch, one of the most powerful beings, battles her grief and destiny, unleashing chaos in the Multiverse of Madness.

Spider-Man

Peter Parker's journey through responsibility and loss reaches new heights as he faces the Multiverse and unites with alternate Spider-Men in No Way Home.

Black Panther

King T'Challa protects Wakanda with honor and wisdom. His legacy continues in Wakanda Forever, where the mantle is passed to a new protector.

Thor

The God of Thunder, wielder of Mjolnir and Stormbreaker, brings might and charm to the Avengers. His cosmic adventures continue across the galaxy.

Loki

The mischievous brother of Thor evolves from villain to anti-hero, exploring his identity and fate across timelines in the Multiverse.

Ant-Man

Scott Lang uses shrinking technology for heists and heroism. He explores the Quantum Realm and faces Kang in Quantumania.

Hawkeye

Clint Barton, the master archer, mentors Kate Bishop while confronting ghosts from his past as Ronin. Loyal, focused, and grounded.

Shang-Chi

Master of martial arts and wielder of the Ten Rings, Shang-Chi brings ancient powers into modern battles, redefining the MCU's mystic side.

Captain Marvel

Carol Danvers is a cosmic powerhouse protecting the universe. Her strength and leadership make her a crucial player in cosmic wars.

Guardians of the Galaxy

Led by Star-Lord, the Guardians are a ragtag team of cosmic heroes who bring humor, heart, and heroism to the MCU's galactic saga.

Moon Knight

Marc Spector is a vigilante with dissociative identity disorder who becomes the avatar of the Egyptian god Khonshu, embracing justice through mysticism and brutal force.

Ms. Marvel

Kamala Khan is a Pakistani-American teen from Jersey City with shape-shifting powers who idolizes Captain Marvel and discovers her mutant heritage.

Vision

A synthezoid created from Vibranium and the Mind Stone, Vision explores love, logic, and humanity—especially in WandaVision.

Sam Wilson

From winged Avenger to the new Captain America, Sam Wilson steps into the legacy of Steve Rogers with honor, courage, and heart.

Bucky Barnes

Once the brainwashed Winter Soldier, Bucky seeks redemption as a super-soldier with a dark past and a new purpose alongside Sam Wilson.

She-Hulk

Jennifer Walters, a lawyer and cousin of Bruce Banner, juggles superpowers and courtrooms in a fun, fourth-wall-breaking style.

Deadpool

The merc with a mouth. Wade Wilson is an unpredictable, regenerative antihero with a twisted sense of humor and a knack for breaking the fourth wall.

Wolverine

Logan is a mutant with animal-like senses, enhanced physical capabilities, and a powerful healing factor. His adamantium claws are legendary.

Mr. Fantastic

Reed Richards, the brilliant leader of the Fantastic Four, can stretch his body to incredible lengths. A genius scientist with a heart for discovery.

Invisible Woman

Susan Storm can become invisible and create force fields. She's the emotional core of the Fantastic Four and a powerful force in her own right.

Human Torch

Johnny Storm ignites into flames and flies with ease. His fiery personality and blazing powers make him one of Marvel’s most energetic heroes.

The Thing

Ben Grimm's rock-like appearance and super strength make him a powerful member of the Fantastic Four. Known for his catchphrase: "It's clobberin' time!"
