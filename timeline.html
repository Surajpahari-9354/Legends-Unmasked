MCU Timeline

1943 - The First Avenger

Steve Rogers becomes Captain America during World War II after being injected with the Super Soldier Serum.

1963 - The Birth of S.H.I.E.L.D.

Nick Fury and Peggy Carter help establish the organization that would later monitor superhuman activity globally.

1980 - Ego Plants the Seed

Ego, the living planet and father of Peter Quill, travels across galaxies planting seeds to expand himself.

1995 - Captain Marvel's Arrival

Carol Danvers gains incredible cosmic powers and helps defend Earth from an alien invasion.

2008 - The Rise of Iron Man

Tony Stark builds the Iron Man suit and declares himself a superhero, beginning the MCU.

2012 - The Battle of New York

The Avengers assemble for the first time to stop Loki and the Chitauri army from invading Earth.

2014 - Guardians of the Galaxy

Star-Lord forms a ragtag team of misfits to stop Ronan the Accuser from using an Infinity Stone.

2016 - Civil War

The Avengers split into factions after the Sokovia Accords divide their opinions on government control.

2018 - Infinity War

Thanos collects all six Infinity Stones and snaps away half the universe’s population.

2023 - Endgame & Time Heist

The Avengers travel back in time to retrieve the Infinity Stones and reverse Thanos’s snap.

2024 - Multiverse Cracks

Events in Loki, Spider-Man: No Way Home, and Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness lead to the fracturing of the multiverse.

2025 - Kang the Conqueror Emerges

After the multiversal war, Kang appears as the new cosmic threat, teased across several realities.

2025 - Moon Knight's Awakening

Marc Spector, chosen by the Egyptian god Khonshu, battles inner demons and enemies of the night.

2026 - Secret Wars on the Horizon

The multiverse spirals into chaos, leading to an ultimate battle among realities — Secret Wars is foretold.
